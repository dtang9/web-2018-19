How to pay using Google Pay


Step 1:

Create a Gmail account if you don't have one to pay using Google Pay. Use this link to sign into Google Pay or create an account: Google Pay

Step 2:

  • Click on "Send or request money" and enter the information as below (Note: Your amount may vary between $10 and $5 depending on which membership you choose. For a list of memberships and benefits click here)

  • Click on Send

    • Make sure you enter your first name and last name in the Memo section.

    Step 3:

  • After you have paid, you will receive a payment confirmation in the form of a receipt in your email which will look like the image below.

  • Take a screenshot of this receipt using [Alt + PrtScn] for Windows or [Cmd + Shift + 3] for Mac.

    Step 4:

    Upload this screenshot on your ACM membership application in the last step.
