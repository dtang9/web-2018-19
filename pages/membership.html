How do I get involved?


The club holds many events throughout the semester. However, one of most important experiences is being involved with the workshops. To get the most benefits off being a member of the club - make sure to meet deadlines. Be aware that workshop space is very limited, so don't miss the first meeting of each semester (check the calendar for more details). There are two types of annual memberships...

5$ membership

  • ❖ Opportunity to join hands-on work shops - access to all equipment.
  • ❖ Complementary pizza every meeting.
  • ❖ Access to all presentations with speakers from companies like google, NASA, LA County, and more!
  • ❖ Tutoring/Extra credit opportunities.

10$ membership

  • ❖ Develop skills with Project experience through Intensive Workshops
  • ❖ Get all the benefits of the basic pack, with added bonuses.
  • ❖ ACM Membership Card/ID & ACM Shirt
  • ❖ Free food to whatever we're fundraising (ACM Membership Card required).
